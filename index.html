Why Paris

Paris provides many opportunities for learning, leisure time and culturally enriching expeirences!

Paris is home to some of the worlds top institutions like University of Paris, Sorbonne Universtiy and University of Paris-Saclay, arife with commodities and many cultural and historical sites like the louvre, notre dam and many, many more

Choosing a school

The right school can make all the differences, and thankfully Paris has top of the line schools for all fields, technology, arts, sports and everything else you can imagine! This means that in paris anyone can soar above the rest!

Culture

In paris there are many sites you can go to to experience the thick, rich culture of Paris, one such site bing the louvre, a place were Paris arts truly come to life. Additionally Paris has many other sites you can visit such as there all to famous Eiffel Tower, or the Notre Dam!

Options

In Paris you can have almost all the freedom you want when it comes to education, some schools you can go to are Sorbonne, Assas, Paris Cité, Sciences Po, ENS, Polytechnique and HEC, these schools have programs that allow everyone to indulge their unique talents and truly rise above the rest of the crowd.

Leisure

In Paris you can enjoy almost every kind of student entertainment imaginable, from late-night concerts, cinemas, and theater shows to festivals, museums, and cultural events, all of which let university students explore, socialize, and make memories beyond the classroom.

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a global symbol of Paris and a testament to creativity. For students, it represents a city where ideas rise as high as its structure. A landmark of inspiration, culture, and history.

The Louvre

Home to thousands of masterpieces, the Louvre reflects Paris’s roots in art and knowledge. It inspires students to delve into creativity and broaden their perspectives every day.

Art & Creativity

Fostering artistic expression at every corner, Paris is home to modern galleries and outdoor studios. Students are encouraged to explore their identity, nuture their imagination, and express their ideas.

Fashion & Design

As a global hub for design, Paris thrives on innovation and originallity. Students gain exposure to world-leading fashion houses and cutting-edge studios that redefine creativity.

Student Life

Paris unites cultural backgrounds and celebrates diversity. Students find friendships, community, and shared experiences that help them grow personally and academically in a welcoming global environment.

Heritage & Architecture

A city where centuries-old heritage meets modern urban life, Paris' iconic architecture reminds all students to look at life from different perspectives - literally and figuratively!
